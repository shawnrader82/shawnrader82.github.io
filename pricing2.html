What affects pricing

How our notary and apostille fees work

Most fees depend on the type of appointment, number of notarized signatures, travel distance, facility rules, and whether you need standard or rush handling.

This page shows our standard pricing structure for common appointments. Before we confirm your booking, we review the location, number of signers, travel, and any special needs so you understand the expected total in advance.

If your situation involves multiple stops, urgent timing, a hospital bedside signing, a jail facility, or a large apostille batch, we may give you a custom quote instead of forcing your order into a generic fee.

For service details beyond pricing, visit our mobile notary services, hospital notary, real estate notary, and apostille services pages.

General mobile notary

Standard mobile notary pricing

  • Standard mobile notary visit: $80, includes the first notarized signature.
  • Additional notarized signatures: $15 each during the same visit.
  • Additional travel: + $10 for each additional 10 miles of round-trip travel beyond the standard area.
  • After-hours / emergency surcharge: usually + $50, confirmed before booking.

Most everyday appointments fall into this category, including school forms, affidavits, travel consent forms, simple powers of attorney, and other routine notarizations at homes, offices, or agreed meeting locations.

If you are not sure whether your documents count as a general mobile notary visit or a specialty appointment, send us a photo and we will tell you before you book.
