Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mobile American Notary & Apostilles (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or contact us for services in Los Angeles, California.

Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Contact details such as your name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • Appointment details such as service type, preferred time, and service location.
  • Limited technical data such as your IP address and basic browser information.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule appointments.
  • Provide notary, apostille, and related services you request.
  • Maintain basic business records and comply with legal obligations.

SMS and phone numbers

If you provide your mobile number and consent to receive SMS, we will use your number to send appointment-related text messages, such as confirmations, reminders, and a one-time review request after your appointment. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP to opt out at any time or HELP for help. We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third‑party providers only as needed to:

  • Process payments or manage bookings.
  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal requests.

Data retention and security

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for business and legal purposes and take reasonable measures to protect it against unauthorized access or disclosure.
