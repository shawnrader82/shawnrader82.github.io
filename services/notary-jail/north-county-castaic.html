About North County Correctional Facility (NCCF)

North County Correctional Facility is a maximum-security complex within the Pitchess Detention Center in Castaic, approximately 40 miles north of downtown Los Angeles. NCCF consists of five housing pods and is one of four facilities on the 2,620-acre Pitchess property.

  • Address: 29340 The Old Road, Castaic, CA 91384
  • Watch commander: (661) 295‑7804
  • Main control: (661) 295‑7810
  • Inmate information (24‑hr): (213) 473‑6080
  • Operated by: Los Angeles County Sheriff’s Department
  • Capacity: Approximately 4,295 inmates
  • Professional visiting hours: Monday–Friday, 7:00 AM – 8:00 PM
  • Parking: Visitor parking at the Dr. Elmer T. Jafee lot; shuttle bus to facility

Why inmates at NCCF need notary services

  • Longer sentences — Inmates often need to delegate authority to family members or attorneys for finances, property, and ongoing legal matters.
  • Property and vehicle issues — Signing deeds, titles, and other documents so family can sell or manage property, vehicles, or businesses.
  • Estate and family planning — Powers of attorney, wills (with attorney help), and other planning documents.
  • Active legal cases — Declarations, affidavits, and other documents that must be signed in front of a notary.

How it works

Scheduling a Notary Appointment at North County

1. Contact us with the inmate’s information

Call or text with the inmate’s full legal name, booking number (if known), and a description of the documents that need to be notarized. Let us know that they are at North County Correctional Facility, if you already have that information.

2. We confirm housing and visiting procedures

NCCF has its own schedule and rules for professional visits. We confirm that the inmate is currently housed at North County and review the facility’s procedures so the appointment follows their requirements.

3. Prepare your documents

Have all documents ready and complete before the appointment — we cannot draft legal documents, only notarize signatures. If the inmate needs to review documents first, coordinate with their attorney or mail the documents to the facility in advance.

4. The notary appointment in Castaic

We travel to North County, clear security, and meet with the inmate in the designated professional visiting area. The inmate confirms their identity, signs the documents in our presence, and we complete the notarization with the appropriate certificate.

5. Document delivery

After the appointment, we return the signed and notarized documents to you, your attorney, escrow company, or other designated recipient — by hand delivery in the LA area, courier, or mail depending on your timeline.

Common Questions

North County Correctional Facility Notary FAQs

How do I confirm someone is at North County Correctional Facility?

You can search the LA County Sheriff’s Inmate Information Center online at app5.lasd.org/iic using the inmate’s name and date of birth. The system will list the current facility, including North County if that is where they are housed.

Can you visit both North County and Pitchess on the same day?

In some situations, yes. If you have documents for inmates at both North County and a Pitchess unit, let us know. We can discuss whether it is practical to coordinate both visits on the same trip, subject to facility schedules.

Are visiting rules different at North County?

North County is a maximum‑security facility with its own visiting rules and procedures for professional visits. We confirm current requirements with the facility to ensure your appointment follows their guidelines.

How far in advance should I schedule an NCCF appointment?

Because of security and travel time, we recommend contacting us at least a few days in advance when possible. For urgent matters, call or text as soon as you know you need the appointment and we will review options.

What ID does the inmate need?

NCCF maintains booking records with photos that can be used for identification purposes. If the inmate has a valid California ID, driver’s license, or passport on file, that can also be used. We confirm ID options before the appointment.

What if the inmate is transferred to another facility?

Inmates can be transferred between Castaic and downtown facilities. We recommend confirming the inmate’s location close to the appointment time. If they have been moved, we can usually reschedule for the new facility with an updated quote if travel distance changes.
