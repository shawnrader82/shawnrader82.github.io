About the James A. Musick Facility

The James A. Musick Facility — sometimes called “The Farm” — is a minimum-security jail in Irvine operated by the Orange County Sheriff's Department. With approximately 1,250 beds, it houses both male and female inmates who are typically low-risk individuals convicted of minor offenses and non-violent misdemeanors, as well as some inmates awaiting arraignment.

  • Address: 13502 Musick Road, Irvine, CA 92618
  • Main phone: (949) 855‑2600
  • Visiting information: (949) 249‑5059
  • Operated by: Orange County Sheriff's Department
  • Security level: Minimum security
  • Capacity: Approximately 1,250 beds
  • Public visiting hours: Friday – Monday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
  • Attorney visits: 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • Location: South Orange County, off the I-5 and I-405 in Irvine

How it works

Scheduling a Notary Appointment at the Musick Facility

1. Contact us with the inmate's information

Call or text with the inmate's full legal name, booking number (if known), and a description of the documents that need to be notarized. Let us know your deadline so we can prioritize scheduling.

2. We coordinate with Musick

We confirm current visiting procedures, professional visit scheduling, and ensure the inmate is available and housed at the Musick Facility (not temporarily transferred for court or medical reasons).

3. Prepare your documents

Have all documents ready and complete before the appointment — we cannot draft legal documents, only notarize signatures. If the inmate needs to review documents first, coordinate with their attorney or use the facility's mail system.

4. The notary appointment

We travel from Los Angeles to the Musick Facility in Irvine, check in with facility staff, and meet with the inmate. The inmate confirms their identity, signs the documents in our presence, and we complete the notarization.

5. Document delivery

After the appointment, we return the signed and notarized documents to you, your attorney, escrow company, or other designated recipient — by hand delivery, courier, or mail depending on your timeline.

Common Questions

Musick Facility Notary FAQs

Where is the James A. Musick Facility?

The Musick Facility is located at 13502 Musick Road, Irvine, CA 92618, in south Orange County near the junction of the I-5 and I-405 freeways.

What kind of inmates are housed at Musick?

Musick primarily houses low-risk inmates, including those convicted of minor offenses and non-violent misdemeanors. It houses both male and female inmates and has a capacity of approximately 1,250 beds.

How do I find out if someone is at the Musick Facility?

You can search the Orange County Sheriff's Department inmate locator online or call the facility at (949) 855‑2600 with the inmate's name and date of birth.

What ID does the inmate need for notarization?

The facility maintains booking records with photos that can be used for identification. Acceptable government-issued IDs include a California driver's license, California ID card, U.S. passport, alien registration card, or military ID.

Is visiting at Musick different from other OC jails?

Yes. Musick is a minimum-security facility, so the visiting environment is generally more relaxed than at maximum-security jails. Visits take place at benches rather than behind glass. Attorneys and other professional visitors can visit 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

How long does a notary appointment at Musick take?

Plan for 2 to 3 hours total including travel from Los Angeles to south Irvine, check-in, wait time, and the signing. The notarization itself is quick, but the distance from LA adds significant travel time.
