About the Theo Lacy Facility

The Theo Lacy Facility is a maximum-security jail complex situated in the City of Orange, operated by the Orange County Sheriff's Department. With a capacity of approximately 3,100 to 3,400 inmates, it is one of the largest jail facilities in Orange County. Theo Lacy houses both sentenced and pre-sentenced male inmates arrested for misdemeanors and felonies.

Scheduling a Notary Appointment at Theo Lacy

  1. Contact us with the inmate's information

    Call or text with the inmate's full legal name, booking number (if known), and a description of the documents that need to be notarized. Let us know your deadline so we can prioritize scheduling.

  2. We coordinate with Theo Lacy

    The Theo Lacy Facility has specific procedures for professional visits. We confirm the current visiting schedule, any advance requirements, and ensure the inmate is available and housed at Theo Lacy.

  3. Prepare your documents

    Have all documents ready and complete before the appointment — we cannot draft legal documents, only notarize signatures. If the inmate needs to review documents first, coordinate with their attorney or use the facility's mail system.

  4. The notary appointment

    We travel from Los Angeles to the Theo Lacy Facility in Orange, clear security screening, and meet with the inmate in the designated professional visiting area. The inmate confirms their identity, signs the documents in our presence, and we complete the notarization.

  5. Document delivery

    After the appointment, we return the signed and notarized documents to you, your attorney, escrow company, or other designated recipient — by hand delivery, courier, or mail depending on your timeline.

Tip: Theo Lacy is a busy facility with thousands of inmates. Professional visit scheduling can fill up — give us as much notice as possible, but we understand jail notarizations are often urgent and will do our best to accommodate same-day or next-day requests.

Theo Lacy Notary FAQs

Where is the Theo Lacy Facility?

The Theo Lacy Facility is located at 501 The City Drive South, Orange, CA 92868, between the Santa Ana Freeway (I-5) and the Garden Grove Freeway (SR-22). Parking is available at the North parking structure across from the Lamoreaux Justice Center.

How many inmates does Theo Lacy hold?

Theo Lacy is a maximum-security facility with a capacity of approximately 3,100 to 3,400 inmates. It houses both sentenced and pre-sentenced male inmates arrested for misdemeanors and felonies.

How do I find out if someone is at Theo Lacy?

You can search the Orange County Sheriff's Department inmate locator online or call the facility at (714) 935-6940 with the inmate's name and date of birth.

What ID does the inmate need for notarization?

The facility maintains booking records with photos that can be used for identification. If the inmate has a valid California ID, driver's license, or passport on file, that can also be used. We confirm ID options before the appointment.

Can you visit Theo Lacy any day for notarizations?

Professional visitors such as attorneys and notaries typically have broader access than public visitors, who are limited to Friday through Monday. We confirm the current professional visiting schedule with the facility before each appointment.

How long does a notary appointment at Theo Lacy take?

Plan for 1 to 3 hours total including travel from Los Angeles, security screening, wait time, and the signing. The notarization itself is quick, but facility procedures and travel from LA add time.
