About the Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility

The Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility is located at 1627 South Hargrave Street in Banning, California, near the Interstate 10 freeway in the San Gorgonio Pass area. The facility is operated by the Riverside County Sheriff’s Department and primarily houses sentenced inmates serving county time, as well as some pre‑trial detainees.

  • Address: 1627 S Hargrave Street, Banning, CA 92220
  • Operated by: Riverside County Sheriff’s Department

  • Housing: Multiple housing units, each with its own visiting schedule

How it works

Scheduling a Notary Appointment at Smith Correctional

1. Contact us with the inmate’s information

Call or text with the inmate’s full legal name, booking number (if known), housing unit if available, and a description of the documents that need to be notarized. Let us know your deadline so we can prioritize scheduling.

2. We schedule with the facility

Riverside County facilities require visits to be scheduled at least one day in advance. For attorney or professional visits at Smith Correctional, we contact the facility directly at (951) 922‑7300 to coordinate scheduling and confirm the correct visiting window for the inmate’s housing unit.

3. Prepare your documents

Have all documents ready and complete before the appointment — we cannot draft legal documents, only notarize signatures. If the inmate needs to review documents first, coordinate with their attorney or use the facility’s mail system.

4. The notary appointment in Banning

We travel from Los Angeles to Smith Correctional in Banning (approximately 1.5 hours), clear security screening, and meet with the inmate in the designated visiting area. Visitor check‑in begins 60 minutes before and closes 20 minutes before the start time, so we plan to arrive early. The inmate confirms their identity, signs the documents, and we complete the notarization.

5. Document delivery

After the appointment, we return the signed and notarized documents to you, your attorney, escrow company, or other designated recipient — by hand delivery, courier, or mail depending on your timeline.

Common Questions

Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility Notary FAQs

Where is the Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility located?

Smith Correctional is located at 1627 S Hargrave Street, Banning, CA 92220, near the I‑10 freeway in the San Gorgonio Pass area. It is approximately 1.5 hours from Los Angeles by car.

How do I confirm someone is at Smith Correctional?

You can search the Riverside County Sheriff’s inmate locator online or call the facility at (951) 922‑7300 with the inmate’s name and date of birth to confirm they are housed at Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility.

How are visits scheduled at Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility?

You can call (951) 922‑7540 between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM daily to schedule visits, and visits must be scheduled at least one day in advance. For attorney or professional visits, the facility is contacted at (951) 922‑7300 to coordinate directly.

What are the check‑in rules for visits?

Visitor check‑in begins 60 minutes before the scheduled visit and closes 20 minutes before the start time. Visitors who arrive during the final 20 minutes before the visit will not be allowed to check in, so arriving early is essential.

What ID does the inmate need for notarization?

The facility maintains booking records that can be used for identification. If the inmate has a valid California ID, driver’s license, or passport on file, that can also be used. We confirm ID options with the facility before the appointment.

How long should I plan for a Smith Correctional notary appointment?

Plan for 4 to 5 hours total including travel from Los Angeles (approximately 1.5 hours each way), security screening, wait time, and the signing. The notarization itself is quick, but travel and facility procedures add significant time.
