About Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility

The Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility is located at 1627 South Hargrave Street in Banning, California, near the Interstate 10 freeway in the San Gorgonio Pass area. The facility is operated by the Riverside County Sheriff's Department and primarily houses sentenced inmates serving county time, as well as some pre-trial detainees.

Smith Correctional is a significant drive from Los Angeles, approximately 1.5 hours via I-10 through the Inland Empire. The facility includes multiple housing units with their own visiting schedules. Visitor check-in begins 60 minutes before the visit start time and ends 20 minutes before, so planning arrival carefully is important.

Scheduling a Notary Appointment at Smith Correctional

  1. Contact us with the inmate's information

    Call or text with the inmate's full legal name, booking number (if known), and a description of the documents that need to be notarized. Let us know your deadline so we can prioritize scheduling.

  2. We coordinate with the facility

    Riverside County facilities require visits to be scheduled at least one day in advance. For attorney or professional visits at Smith Correctional, we contact the facility directly at (951) 922-7300 to coordinate scheduling. Due to the remote Banning location, we plan the trip carefully to ensure timely arrival.

  3. Prepare your documents

    Have all documents ready and complete before the appointment — we cannot draft legal documents, only notarize signatures. If the inmate needs to review documents first, coordinate with their attorney or use the facility's mail system.

  4. The notary appointment

    We travel from Los Angeles to Smith Correctional in Banning (approximately 1.5 hours), clear security screening, and meet with the inmate in the designated visiting area. Visitor check-in begins 60 minutes before and closes 20 minutes before the start time, so we plan to arrive early. The inmate confirms their identity, signs the documents, and we complete the notarization.

  5. Document delivery

    After the appointment, we return the signed and notarized documents to you, your attorney, escrow company, or other designated recipient — by hand delivery, courier, or mail depending on your timeline.

Tip: Smith Correctional has multiple housing units, each with their own visiting schedule. When you contact us, provide the inmate's housing unit or booking number so we can coordinate the correct visiting window. Visitor check-in closes 20 minutes before the scheduled start time, so arriving late means a wasted trip.

Smith Correctional Notary FAQs

Where is the Larry D. Smith Correctional Facility?

Smith Correctional is located at 1627 S Hargrave St, Banning, CA 92220, near the I-10 freeway in the San Gorgonio Pass area. It is approximately 1.5 hours from Los Angeles.

How do I find out if someone is at Smith Correctional?

You can search the Riverside County Sheriff's inmate locator online or call (951) 922-7300 with the inmate's name and date of birth.

How do I schedule a visit at Smith Correctional?

Call (951) 922-7540 between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM daily. Visits must be scheduled at least one day in advance. For attorney or professional visits, contact the facility at (951) 922-7300.

What are the check-in rules at Smith Correctional?

Visitor check-in begins 60 minutes before the scheduled visit and closes 20 minutes before the start time. Visitors who arrive during the final 20 minutes will not be allowed to visit. Plan to arrive early.

What ID does the inmate need for notarization?

The facility maintains booking records that can be used for identification. If the inmate has a valid California ID, driver's license, or passport on file, that can also be used.

How long does a notary appointment at Smith Correctional take?

Plan for 4 to 5 hours total including travel from Los Angeles (approximately 1.5 hours each way), security screening, wait time, and the signing.
