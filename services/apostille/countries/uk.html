UK visa & immigration

UKVI requires apostilled birth and marriage certificates for Settlement Visas, Indefinite Leave to Remain, and family reunification applications. We confirm the correct certified copy format for each application type.

2

Adults born in the U.S. to British parents claiming citizenship through descent must submit an apostilled U.S. birth certificate alongside the British parent’s own documentation to the Home Office or HM Passport Office.

Process

How UK apostille service works.

We confirm the correct document format and certified copy first, then route through the California Secretary of State apostille so your document arrives in the UK ready for UKVI, HM Land Registry, your NHS employer, or your UK solicitor.

Step 1 — Review the document and UK purpose

  • Send a photo of your birth certificate, marriage certificate, degree, or power of attorney draft.
  • Tell us which UK authority or entity (UKVI, Home Office, NMC, HM Land Registry, solicitor) will receive it.
  • We confirm the correct certified copy format — long-form vs. short-form, original vs. notarized copy — before you mail anything.

Step 2 — Confirm the correct apostille authority

  • California-issued documents: apostilled by the California Secretary of State.
  • Other U.S. state-issued documents: apostilled by that state’s Secretary of State.
  • Federal documents (e.g., Consular Report of Birth Abroad): apostilled by the U.S. Department of State.

Step 3 — Submit for apostille

  • We submit your certified document to the California Secretary of State and track the order through processing (typically 2–5 business days).
  • Rush apostille service is available when UK visa appointments or property completions are imminent.

Step 4 — Ship to you or your UK recipient

  • We ship the apostilled document to you in Los Angeles, to your UK solicitor, to a UKVI application centre, or to family in the UK — wherever it is needed.
  • Tracked international courier to the UK typically takes 3–5 business days.

UK apostille FAQ

Questions about apostilling documents for the United Kingdom.

Does the UK accept a California apostille?

Yes. The United Kingdom is a member of the Hague Apostille Convention. A California Secretary of State apostille on a U.S.-origin document is the correct and sufficient authentication for use with UKVI, the Home Office, HM Land Registry, NHS registration bodies, UK solicitors, and UK courts. No additional U.S. Embassy or UK Consulate legalization is required.

Who issues the apostille for documents going to the UK?

For U.S.-origin documents used in the UK, the apostille is issued by the relevant U.S. state’s Secretary of State (for state-issued documents, such as California birth or marriage certificates) or the U.S. Department of State (for federal documents, such as a Consular Report of Birth Abroad). The UK FCDO Legalisation Office in Milton Keynes issues apostilles on UK-origin documents — that is a separate process we do not handle.

Can I apostille my U.S. degree for NHS registration or UK teaching credentials?

Yes. U.S.-trained healthcare professionals applying to the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC), General Medical Council (GMC), or other UK health regulators, and teachers applying for UK Qualified Teacher Status (QTS), regularly need apostilled U.S. degrees. We apostille the original diploma or a notarized certified copy through the California Secretary of State.

Do I need a UK solicitor to prepare my power of attorney before you notarize it?

For UK property transactions, HM Land Registry filings, or UK banking, we strongly recommend having a UK solicitor draft or review the power of attorney. UK legal requirements for conveyancing and Land Registry execution differ from U.S. forms. We add the California notarial block to the UK-standard document and route it through the California Secretary of State for apostille — which the UK solicitor then uses directly.

How long does a UK apostille take from Los Angeles?

California Secretary of State apostille typically takes 2–5 business days. Rush apostille service is available in 1–2 business days when UK visa appointments or property completions are imminent. Shipping to the UK adds 3–5 business days via tracked courier. Total turnaround from receiving your document is usually within one week for standard service.
