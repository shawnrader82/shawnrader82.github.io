Before your appointment

What to have ready for a bedside notary visit.

✓

Valid photo ID

Every signer needs a current government-issued photo ID — California driver's license, state ID, U.S. passport, or equivalent. If the patient's ID is at home, have a family member bring it before the appointment.

✓

All document pages

Bring complete documents — not just the signature page. If you need documents generated on-site, we bring a laptop, portable printer, and internet connection to produce forms during our visit.

✓

Any required witnesses

Healthcare directives typically require two witnesses who are not named in the document and are not employees of the treating facility. Confirm witness requirements for your specific document before we arrive.

Mobile Notary FAQ for Providence Tarzana

Frequently asked questions.

Is this the same hospital as Cedars-Sinai in Beverly Grove?

No. Providence Cedars-Sinai Tarzana Medical Center is a separate 204-bed hospital in the San Fernando Valley, affiliated with both the Providence health system and Cedars-Sinai. It is a different facility from the main Cedars-Sinai Medical Center at 8700 Beverly Boulevard in Beverly Grove.

How quickly can you get to Providence Tarzana from Granada Hills?

Providence Tarzana is one of our closer hospitals. Travel time is typically 20 to 35 minutes via Reseda Boulevard or the 101 freeway. During business hours, we can often arrive within 1 to 2 hours for urgent requests.

What are visiting hours at Providence Cedars-Sinai Tarzana?

Providence Cedars-Sinai Tarzana supports open visitation, allowing visitors at any time. Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM.

Can you notarize documents for a patient in the NICU or maternity ward?

Yes. Providence Tarzana has the largest Level III NICU in the San Fernando Valley. We can visit parents in the NICU or maternity unit to notarize powers of attorney, guardianship forms, or other legal documents.

What if the patient came in through the emergency room and is now admitted?

Once a patient is admitted from the ER and assigned a room, we can schedule a visit. If the patient is still being treated in the ER, notarization may be more difficult due to movement and limited privacy — we recommend waiting until the patient is in an inpatient room.

Do you also serve Encino Hospital Medical Center nearby?

Yes. Encino Hospital Medical Center on Ventura Boulevard is close to Providence Tarzana, and we serve both facilities at Tier 1 rates.

Can the hospital social worker arrange a notary instead?

Some hospitals have occasional access to notary services through social work or patient services departments, but availability is often limited. Families frequently call us directly because we can respond quickly and handle all document types.
