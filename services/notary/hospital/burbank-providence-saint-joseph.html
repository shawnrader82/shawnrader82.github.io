Before your appointment

What to have ready for a bedside notary visit.

✓

Valid photo ID

Every signer needs a current government-issued photo ID — California driver's license, state ID, U.S. passport, or equivalent. If the patient's ID is at home, have a family member bring it before the appointment.

✓

All document pages

Bring complete documents — not just the signature page. If you need documents generated on-site, we bring a laptop, portable printer, and internet connection to produce forms during our visit.

✓

Any required witnesses

Healthcare directives typically require two witnesses who are not named in the document and are not employees of the treating facility. Confirm witness requirements for your specific document before we arrive.

Mobile Notary FAQ for Providence Saint Joseph

Frequently asked questions.

Can you notarize documents at the Disney Family Cancer Center?

Yes. The Roy and Patricia Disney Family Cancer Center at 181 S. Buena Vista Blvd. is on the Providence Saint Joseph campus. Cancer patients often need powers of attorney, healthcare directives, or other legal documents — we can come to the cancer center directly.

What are visiting hours at Providence Saint Joseph Medical Center?

Patient visitation is available 24 hours a day. Visitors arriving between 7 PM and 7 AM enter through the Emergency Department and check in with the desk clerk on duty. All visitors must check in at the main entrance between 7 AM and 7 PM.

How long does it take to get to Providence Saint Joseph from Granada Hills?

Travel time to Burbank typically runs 25 to 45 minutes depending on traffic on the 118, 5, and 134 freeways. Rush hour or accidents can add significant time. We always check current conditions before confirming your arrival window.

What if the patient is not fully alert due to serious illness or medication?

We must confirm that the signer understands what they are signing and is acting willingly. For patients dealing with serious illness, pain, or medication effects, we recommend scheduling during a time when the patient is most alert, often in the morning.

Can family members from out of town be present via video call during the signing?

Family members can observe via video call for their own peace of mind, but only the people physically present can sign documents or serve as witnesses. If a family member needs to sign as well, they would need a separate notarization in their own location.

Do you also serve Glendale Adventist or other nearby hospitals?

Yes, we serve hospitals throughout the Glendale and Burbank area. If your family member is at a different facility nearby, contact us and we can discuss availability and pricing.

Is there anything specific about Providence hospitals I should know for a notary visit?

Providence facilities generally have straightforward visitor policies. If the patient is in a restricted area like the ICU or a specialized unit, confirm with the nursing staff before we arrive so we can coordinate the visit smoothly.
