Before your appointment

What to have ready for a bedside notary visit.

✓

Valid photo ID

Every signer needs a current government-issued photo ID — California driver's license, state ID, U.S. passport, or equivalent. If the patient's ID is at home, have a family member bring it before the appointment.

✓

All document pages

Bring complete documents — not just the signature page. If you need documents generated on-site, we bring a laptop, portable printer, and internet connection to produce forms during our visit.

✓

Any required witnesses

Healthcare directives typically require two witnesses who are not named in the document and are not employees of the treating facility. Confirm witness requirements for your specific document before we arrive.

Mobile Notary FAQ for Henry Mayo

Frequently asked questions.

How long does it take you to get to Henry Mayo from Granada Hills?

Travel time to Valencia depends heavily on I-5 traffic through the Newhall Pass. During off-peak hours, we can typically arrive in 30 to 45 minutes. During rush hour or after an accident, it can take an hour or more.

What are visiting hours at Henry Mayo Newhall Hospital?

Henry Mayo offers 24/7 visitation with a few exceptions. Most units allow two visitors at a time. The Emergency Department allows one visitor per patient. All visitors must check in at the hospital entrance.

Is Henry Mayo the only hospital you serve in Santa Clarita?

Henry Mayo Newhall Hospital is the primary acute-care hospital in the Santa Clarita Valley. We also serve patients at skilled nursing facilities, rehab centers, and assisted living communities throughout the valley.

Can you notarize documents for a patient being transferred to another facility?

Yes. Transfer situations are common. If a patient is being moved to a rehab center or another hospital, families often need powers of attorney or healthcare directives notarized before the move.

Can you notarize if the patient is in the Henry Mayo emergency room?

Emergency room visits can be challenging due to unpredictable wait times and patient movement. Henry Mayo's ER allows one visitor per patient. If the patient has been admitted to an inpatient room, scheduling is much easier.

What if I need a notary after hours or on a weekend at Henry Mayo?

We offer after-hours and weekend appointments for hospital visits when possible. Henry Mayo allows visitors 24/7, so scheduling flexibility is excellent. Additional fees may apply for evening or weekend requests.

Do you serve other cities in the Santa Clarita Valley besides Valencia?

Yes. While Henry Mayo is in Valencia, we serve patients and families throughout the Santa Clarita Valley, including Newhall, Saugus, Canyon Country, Stevenson Ranch, Castaic, and Agua Dulce.
