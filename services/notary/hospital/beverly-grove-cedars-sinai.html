Before your appointment

What to have ready for a bedside notary visit.

✓

Valid photo ID

Every signer needs a current government-issued photo ID — California driver's license, state ID, U.S. passport, or equivalent. If the patient's ID is at home, have a family member bring it before the appointment.

✓

All document pages

Bring complete documents — not just the signature page. If you need documents generated on-site, we bring a laptop, portable printer, and internet connection to produce forms during our visit.

✓

Any required witnesses

Healthcare directives typically require two witnesses who are not named in the document and are not employees of the treating facility. Confirm witness requirements for your specific document before we arrive.

Mobile Notary FAQ for Cedars-Sinai

Frequently asked questions.

Can you notarize documents in Cedars-Sinai's ICU or specialized units?

Yes — we can visit patients in ICU and specialized units including the Saperstein Critical Care Tower as long as the hospital permits visitors and the patient is alert enough to understand and sign. We coordinate timing with family members and nursing staff to find a window when the patient is stable, awake, and able to participate.

Does Cedars-Sinai provide its own notary service?

Some large hospitals occasionally have internal notary options, but availability is often limited. We operate as an independent mobile notary you hire directly. Families frequently call us when the hospital cannot provide a notary in the needed timeframe or for specific documents like estate-planning forms prepared by an outside attorney.

What are Cedars-Sinai visiting hours?

General visiting hours at Cedars-Sinai are 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Some units may have special hours. We coordinate our arrival within allowed visiting windows or confirm access for professional visits with your family.

How long does it take to get to Cedars-Sinai from Granada Hills?

Travel time to Beverly Grove typically ranges from 35 to 60 minutes depending on traffic on the 101, 405, and surface streets like Beverly, 3rd Street, or Fairfax. During rush hour it can take longer. We always check real-time conditions before confirming your arrival window.

Can you help coordinate with my estate planning attorney?

Yes. Many Cedars patients work with outside attorneys who prepare documents for signature at the hospital. Your attorney can email documents to you or directly to us for printing with our laptop and portable printer. We do not provide legal advice, but we make sure documents are printed, organized, and ready to sign.

What if parking at Cedars-Sinai is full when you arrive?

Parking garages at Cedars can reach capacity at busy times. We allow extra time for parking and may use alternative lots or street parking if needed. We keep you updated by text so you always know our status.

Do you also serve nearby neighborhoods like West Hollywood and Beverly Hills?

Yes. In addition to Cedars-Sinai, we serve patients and families at nearby medical offices, rehab facilities, and residences in Beverly Grove, West Hollywood, Beverly Hills, Carthay, and the surrounding area.
