Before your appointment

What to have ready for a jail notary visit.

✓

Valid photo ID for signers

Every signer needs a current government-issued photo ID. For inmates, jail-issued ID cards are generally acceptable. Confirm the inmate’s current ID status before scheduling — if their ID was booked into property, it may need to be retrieved first.

✓

Complete documents ready to sign

Bring all pages of the document — not just the signature page. Documents should be fully prepared and ready to sign. Missing pages or incomplete forms will require a return visit. We can advise on preparation before we schedule.

✓

Inmate name & booking number

We need the inmate’s full legal name and booking number to confirm their location within the facility. You can typically find the booking number through the county sheriff’s online inmate search or by calling the facility directly.

Mobile Notary FAQ — Southwest Detention Murrieta

Frequently asked questions.

Where is the Southwest Detention Center located?

The Southwest Detention Center is located at 30755-B Auld Rd, Murrieta, CA 92563, in the French Valley area between Murrieta and Temecula. It is approximately 1.5 hours from Los Angeles via the I-15 freeway.

Is Southwest Detention Center the same as the Cois M. Byrd Detention Center?

Yes. The Southwest Detention Center and Cois M. Byrd Detention Center are the same facility, and it may be listed under either name in Riverside County inmate locator systems.

How do I confirm someone is at Southwest / Cois M. Byrd?

You can search the Riverside County Sheriff's inmate locator online or call (951) 696-3050 with the inmate's name and date of birth to confirm they are housed at Southwest Detention Center.

How are visits scheduled at Southwest Detention Center?

You can call (951) 696-3470 between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM daily to schedule visits, and visits must be scheduled at least one day in advance. For attorney or professional visits, contact the facility at (951) 696-3050.

Does Southwest Detention Center house male and female inmates?

No. Southwest Detention Center houses male inmates only. For female inmates in Riverside County, check the Robert Presley Detention Center or contact the Riverside County Sheriff's Department for guidance.

How long should I plan for a Southwest Detention Center notary appointment?

Plan for 4 to 5 hours total including travel from Los Angeles (about 1.5 hours each way via I-15), security screening, wait time, and the signing. The notarization itself is quick, but travel and facility procedures add time.
