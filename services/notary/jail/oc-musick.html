Before your appointment

What to have ready for a jail notary visit.

✓

Valid photo ID for signers

Every signer needs a current government-issued photo ID. For inmates, jail-issued ID cards are generally acceptable. Confirm the inmate’s current ID status before scheduling — if their ID was booked into property, it may need to be retrieved first.

✓

Complete documents ready to sign

Bring all pages of the document — not just the signature page. Documents should be fully prepared and ready to sign. Missing pages or incomplete forms will require a return visit. We can advise on preparation before we schedule.

✓

Inmate name & booking number

We need the inmate’s full legal name and booking number to confirm their location within the facility. You can typically find the booking number through the county sheriff’s online inmate search or by calling the facility directly.

Mobile Notary FAQ — Musick Facility Irvine

Frequently asked questions.

Where is the James A. Musick Facility located?

The James A. Musick Facility is located at 13502 Musick Road, Irvine, CA 92618, in south Orange County near the junction of the I-5 and I-405 freeways.

What type of inmates are housed at Musick?

Musick primarily houses low-risk inmates, including those convicted of minor offenses and non-violent misdemeanors. It houses both male and female inmates and has a capacity of approximately 1,250 beds.

How do I confirm someone is at the Musick Facility?

You can search the Orange County Sheriff's Department inmate locator online or call the facility at (949) 855-2600 with the inmate's name and date of birth.

What ID does the inmate need for notarization at Musick?

The facility maintains booking records with photos that can be used for identification. Acceptable government-issued IDs include a California driver's license, California ID card, U.S. passport, alien registration card, or military ID, when available in the inmate's records.

Is the visiting environment different from other jails?

Yes. Musick is a minimum-security facility, so the visiting environment is generally more relaxed than at maximum-security jails. Visits may take place at benches rather than behind glass, and professional visitors often have more flexible access.

How long should I plan for a Musick notary appointment?

Plan for 2 to 3 hours total including travel from Los Angeles to south Irvine, check-in, wait time, and the signing. The notarization itself is quick, but the distance from LA adds significant travel time.
