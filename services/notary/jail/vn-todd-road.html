Before your appointment

What to have ready for a jail notary visit.

✓

Valid photo ID for signers

Every signer needs a current government-issued photo ID. For inmates, jail-issued ID cards are generally acceptable. Confirm the inmate’s current ID status before scheduling — if their ID was booked into property, it may need to be retrieved first.

✓

Complete documents ready to sign

Bring all pages of the document — not just the signature page. Documents should be fully prepared and ready to sign. Missing pages or incomplete forms will require a return visit. We can advise on preparation before we schedule.

✓

Inmate name & booking number

We need the inmate’s full legal name and booking number to confirm their location within the facility. You can typically find the booking number through the county sheriff’s online inmate search or by calling the facility directly.

Mobile Notary FAQ — Todd Road Jail Santa Paula

Frequently asked questions.

Where is Todd Road Jail located?

Todd Road Jail is located at 600 Todd Road, Santa Paula, CA 93060. It is approximately 70 minutes from Los Angeles via US-101.

How do I confirm someone is housed at Todd Road Jail?

You can search the Ventura County Sheriff's inmate locator online or call (805) 654-3335. Be sure to confirm whether the inmate is at Todd Road or the Main Jail in Ventura, as they are separate facilities.

What are the visiting days and check-in rules at Todd Road?

Visiting days are Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday, and Sunday. Each visit is 30 minutes, with two visits per inmate per week. Check-in starts 1 hour before and closes 30 minutes before the session ends.

What can I bring into Todd Road Jail during a visit?

Only your personal key ring, photo ID, and one baby bottle or blanket if bringing an infant. No cell phones, wallets, purses, backpacks, food, beverages, cameras, or reading materials are allowed. The facility will not hold items and there are no storage lockers.

What is the difference between Todd Road Jail and the Ventura County Main Jail?

The Main Jail in downtown Ventura primarily houses pre-trial detainees and handles initial booking. Todd Road in Santa Paula primarily houses sentenced inmates and those in longer-term custody, with different visiting schedules and housing units.

How long should I plan for a Todd Road notary appointment?

Plan for 3 to 4 hours total including travel from Los Angeles, security screening, wait time, and the signing. Todd Road is approximately 70 minutes each way via US-101.
