Common notarizations at airports and hotels

Many travelers discover at the last minute that they need something notarized. Some of the most common airport notarizations include:

Minor travel consent forms

Parental consent letters for minors traveling with one parent, relatives, or school groups.

Powers of attorney before departure

Financial or general powers of attorney signed right before an extended trip abroad.

Affidavits and sworn statements

Affidavits of support, residency statements, and other declarations required for immigration or travel.

Business & courier documents

Time-sensitive agreements, courier release forms, and other paperwork that must be notarized same-day.
