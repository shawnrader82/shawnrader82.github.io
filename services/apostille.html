Quick FAQ

Common apostille questions

Do I need an apostille or legalization?

It depends on the destination country. Hague countries usually accept an apostille, while non-Hague countries often require authentication plus consular legalization.

Can I use a photocopy?

Usually no. Many documents must be certified copies, original government-issued records, or properly notarized originals before they can be processed.

What is the fastest way to know what I need?

Call or text us with three things: your document type, destination country, and deadline. We will tell you the likely path before you book.
