What to Expect at North County Correctional Facility (NCCF)

Every facility handles inmate marriages differently. Here's what you need to know about getting married at North County Correctional Facility (NCCF).

Approval process: Marriage request submitted through the facility's administrative office. Higher-security classification may result in additional review steps.
Ceremony setting: Ceremonies take place during regular visiting hours. Visiting format may vary based on security classification.
Guest policy: One witness required with valid government-issued photo ID.
Visiting hours: Check current LASD visiting schedules for NCCF. Visiting may be more restricted than at lower-security facilities.
Items allowed: No items may be exchanged. No electronics, cameras, or recording devices.
Good to know: NCCF is adjacent to Pitchess in Castaic, about 40 minutes north of downtown LA. It houses higher-security inmates. Visiting rules and scheduling may be more restrictive than at other LASD facilities. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.

Common Questions

Jail Wedding FAQ — North County Correctional Facility (NCCF)

Is NCCF harder to arrange a wedding at than other jails?

Higher-security facilities can have additional approval steps and more restrictive visiting rules, but weddings are still permitted. We navigate the process for you.

How far is NCCF from downtown LA?

NCCF is in Castaic, about 40 minutes north of downtown LA via the I-5 freeway.

Can the ceremony happen on weekends?

Ceremonies are scheduled during regular visiting hours. Weekend availability depends on the facility's current visiting schedule.

Is NCCF the same as Pitchess?

No. NCCF and Pitchess are adjacent but separate facilities in Castaic. NCCF generally houses higher-security inmates.

What if the inmate is transferred to Pitchess?

We serve both facilities. If there's a transfer, we adjust the process to the new location.

How much does a jail wedding at NCCF cost?

We provide a personalized quote based on your situation. No hidden mileage, after-hours, or weekend surcharges — even for Castaic.
