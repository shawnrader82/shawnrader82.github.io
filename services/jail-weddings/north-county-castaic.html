Getting There & Check‑In

Visiting NCCF for a Wedding

North County Correctional Facility is adjacent to Pitchess Detention Center in Castaic, but operates as a separate higher‑security facility with its own visiting rules.

Directions & Parking

  • Address: 29340 The Old Rd, Castaic, CA 91384 — same general complex as Pitchess, but a separate facility entrance.
  • From the I‑5: Exit at The Old Road in Castaic. Follow signs for NCCF / visitor parking.
  • Drive time: About 40–50 minutes from downtown LA. Weekend mornings on I‑5 north are generally smooth.
  • Parking: Visitor parking at the Dr. Elmer T. Jafee lot, same as Pitchess. Shuttle bus available to the facility.

What to Know Before Check‑In

  • Visiting days: Saturdays and Sundays, 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM. First come, first served — no online scheduling.
  • Arrive very early: NCCF shares the Castaic complex with Pitchess. Lines form well before opening. Be in the parking lot by 6:30–7:00 AM.
  • Higher security: NCCF houses higher‑security inmates. Expect more thorough screening and potentially longer processing times than at Pitchess.
  • Visiting restrictions: Visiting rules may be more restrictive depending on classification. Contact us with the inmate's booking number and we'll confirm what's possible before you make the drive.
  • Bring only: Valid government‑issued photo ID. Leave everything else in your car.
  • Plan for 4+ hours total: Driving, waiting, shuttle, enhanced screening, and the visit itself take most of a morning.
What the Ceremony Is Like

Your Wedding at NCCF

NCCF is a higher‑security facility, which means ceremony logistics can differ from the general population jails.

The Visiting Room

  • Ceremonies take place during regular weekend visiting hours. No private ceremony room is available.
  • Inmates receive one hour of visitation per weekend, split into two 30‑minute sessions.
  • Up to 3 visitors at a time are permitted.
  • The visiting format — glass partition or contact — depends on the inmate's security classification. Higher‑security inmates are more likely to have glass visits.

NCCF‑Specific Considerations

  • Additional approval steps: Higher‑security classification may require extra review for marriage requests. This can add time to the approval process — we factor it in.
  • Restricted housing: Some inmates in restrictive housing may have modified or suspended visiting privileges. Contact us first and we'll determine feasibility.
  • Not the same as Pitchess: Despite sharing the Castaic complex, NCCF and Pitchess are separate facilities with different visiting procedures and approval chains.
  • No items exchanged: Rings, gifts, or documents cannot be passed during the visit.
  • No photography: No phones, cameras, or recording devices inside the facility.
  • Dress code: Dress modestly. No solid blue clothing, revealing outfits, open‑toed shoes, or clothing with offensive imagery.
