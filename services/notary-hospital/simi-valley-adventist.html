About Adventist Health Simi Valley

Bedside notary visits in Simi Valley

How our visits at Adventist Health Simi Valley work

What to expect when you book

We coordinate with you by phone or text to confirm the patient’s legal name, room number, and the documents that need notarization. When we arrive, we check in at the main entrance, obtain a visitor badge, and follow hospital visitor procedures before meeting you in the patient room or a designated family area.

Adventist Health hospitals typically allow visitors from 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM daily. All visitors must check in at the front desk and obtain a badge that remains visible at all times. ICU visitors are requested to wait in the assigned ICU waiting room until cleared by nursing staff. Children under 12 should be accompanied by an adult other than the patient. We schedule our notary visit to fall within visiting hours, and we recommend confirming current policies with the hospital when you book.

Travel to Simi Valley can vary with freeway traffic on the 118. We schedule a specific arrival window that accounts for driving and parking time so you know when to expect us. We will text you when we arrive on campus.

Preparing for your appointment

What to have ready at Adventist Health Simi Valley

To keep your visit smooth, please have:

  • A valid, government-issued photo ID for each signer (driver license, state ID, passport, or other accepted identification)
  • All pages of the documents printed and ready, not just the signature page (unless using our laptop/printer service)
  • Any required witnesses who are not named in the document and who meet the hospital’s visitor rules

If the patient has limited mobility or is unable to sign in their usual way, let us know beforehand so we can discuss acceptable options under California notary law.

If ID is at home, we recommend having a family member bring it to the hospital before the appointment. In some cases, credible witnesses may be an option; we will discuss this with you in advance based on California law.
