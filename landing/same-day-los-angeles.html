Same‑day apostille FAQ

Questions about fast apostille service

Can every document get a same‑day apostille?

Not every document qualifies for same‑day processing. Eligibility depends on the issuing authority, current processing options, and when we receive your paperwork. We will confirm what is possible before you schedule.

Do my documents need to be notarized first?

Many personal and business documents must be notarized before an apostille can be issued, but some government‑issued records can be apostilled without notarization. We will review your documents and let you know what is required.

Can you work with documents from other states?

Yes, we can often coordinate apostilles for documents from other U.S. states, but timing may be longer than same‑ or next‑day depending on that state's rules. We will outline expected timeframes for each jurisdiction.

How do I know if I need an apostille or a different authentication?

Apostilles are used for countries that are part of the Hague Apostille Convention. Other countries may require consular legalization instead. If you tell us which country will use your documents, we will confirm the correct process.
