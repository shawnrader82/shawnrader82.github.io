Common questions

Service area FAQ

A quick orientation to where we travel. If your situation doesn't fit, a call or text is the fastest way to get a clear answer.

Which counties do you cover?

We focus on Los Angeles County and Ventura County. Los Angeles County is our primary service area for general mobile Notary work. In Ventura County, we cover the area inside the 101 / 126 / Los Angeles County corridor, just past the 23 freeway: Conejo Valley (Thousand Oaks, Westlake Village, Newbury Park, Oak Park), Simi Valley, the Moorpark corridor (Moorpark, Somis, Santa Rosa Valley), and the 126 corridor (Piru, Fillmore). For specific hospital, jail, or Apostille needs further out, see our service pages.

Do you offer same-day Notary or Apostille service?

Same-day mobile notarizations are often possible depending on the time of day, traffic, and signer readiness. Same-day Apostille is also possible for documents already correctly notarized or certified, but timing depends on California Secretary of State counter availability. Call or text for the fastest answer.

What if my city isn't listed?

We frequently travel to nearby communities even when they don't have a dedicated page. Call or text with your address, document type, and timing and we'll let you know whether we travel there.
