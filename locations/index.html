Questions before you book?

Common questions about service areas

If you are not sure which city page to choose, start here. We can confirm travel coverage, scheduling options, and whether your appointment involves a home, office, hospital, facility, or detention setting before you book.

How do I know which city or area page to choose?

Pick the city or area closest to where the signing will happen. If your neighborhood is not listed, call or text us and we can confirm the nearest coverage area and whether travel is available for your appointment.

Do you travel to homes, offices, hospitals, and jails?

Yes. Mobile appointments may be available for homes, offices, medical settings, rehab facilities, hospitals, and certain detention-related situations, depending on signer readiness, access rules, timing, and travel distance.

Can I call or text before booking online?

Yes. A quick call or text is often the fastest way to confirm ID requirements, signer availability, document type, travel timing, and whether your situation should be scheduled online or handled directly.

Do you offer same-day or urgent appointments?

Same-day and urgent scheduling may be available depending on your location, time of day, traffic, and the type of appointment. The fastest way to check is to call or text with your city, timing, and document needs.

What if my city is nearby but not shown on this page?

We often serve nearby communities in Los Angeles County, Ventura County, and selected surrounding areas. If you do not see your city listed, contact us and we can let you know whether we currently travel there.
