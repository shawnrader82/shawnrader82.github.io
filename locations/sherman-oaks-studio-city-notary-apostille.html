Documents used outside the U.S.

Apostille help for Sherman Oaks & Studio City residents

When documents from this area need an apostille

Many Sherman Oaks and Studio City families have ties overseas, and entertainment and business professionals here regularly sign contracts, powers of attorney, and corporate resolutions that must be recognized internationally, so it is common for documents to require a California apostille so they are accepted abroad without delays.

Documents that may require an apostille

Typical requests include birth, marriage, and death certificates; school records and transcripts for students studying overseas; powers of attorney for property or business interests abroad; corporate documents for foreign registrations; and court orders related to custody, name changes, or production‑related entities.

How we guide you through the apostille process

We confirm the destination country, review the documents you already have, and explain whether they must be notarized first or obtained from a specific agency before an apostille can be issued, then outline realistic fees and timelines so you can plan around shoot days, work travel, or family schedules.
