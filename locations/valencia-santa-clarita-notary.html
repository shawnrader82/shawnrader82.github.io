Do you charge extra to come to Valencia or Santa Clarita?

Can you help if we are visiting from out of town?

Yes. Many people stay in local hotels near Six Flags Magic Mountain or along Magic Mountain Parkway when visiting family or handling court matters in the Santa Clarita Valley. We can often coordinate notary and, when needed, follow‑up apostille work around your trip.

What identification do we need for a mobile notary appointment?

Each signer must have valid, government‑issued photo ID such as a driver's license or passport, along with all pages of the documents that need to be signed. We review ID questions with you before scheduling.

Can you combine jail signings and other notarizations on the same day?

In some cases we can handle a jail signing at Pitchess and a separate appointment in Valencia or Santa Clarita on the same day, depending on timing and location. We will discuss options, travel time, and fees when you contact us.

How do we know if an apostille will be required?

If your documents will be used in another country, they may require an apostille after issuance or notarization. We can review your situation and confirm whether apostille or consular legalization is necessary as a separate step.
