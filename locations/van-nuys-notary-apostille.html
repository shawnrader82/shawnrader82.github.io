Documents used outside the U.S.

Apostille help for Van Nuys residents & businesses

When Van Nuys documents need an apostille

Many Van Nuys families and business owners maintain ties to other countries or sign agreements with partners abroad, so it is common for documents to require a California apostille so they are accepted internationally without delays.

Documents that may require an apostille

For Van Nuys clients, common requests include birth, marriage, and death certificates; DMV or insurance documents tied to out‑of‑country drivers; school records for international students; powers of attorney for overseas property or business interests; and court orders involving custody or name changes.

How we guide you through the apostille process

We confirm the destination country, review the documents you already have, and explain whether they must be notarized first or obtained from a specific agency before an apostille can be issued, then outline realistic fees and timelines before you decide to move forward.
