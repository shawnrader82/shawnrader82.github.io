How to Use This Library

  1. Click through to the reference page for the form you already know you need.
  2. Download the form directly from the authoritative source linked on that page.
  3. Complete the form yourself, or with a licensed California attorney if you have any legal question about it. We are not able to answer questions about what the form means, which options to select, or what to write in the blanks.
  4. Call or text us when the form is filled out and you are ready to sign in front of a Notary. We come to you.

These forms and reference links are provided free of charge. We do not sell, prepare, review, or complete forms — our fee applies only to the notarial act itself, once the form is ready to be signed. We are not a Legal Document Assistant (LDA) and do not provide LDA services.
